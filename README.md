  ## :pencil: Description

Have you ever watched a movie and liked a song? Filmu is a great place for film and music lovers to add their favorite songs to all their best-loved films. Or they can peruse the songs listed under any of the films featured and leave reviews. As Dorothy and Toto once said, "There's no place like Filmu!"

## :fire: Getting Started

<details open>
  <summary> How to Create a Movie </summary>
    1. Insert title of movie.
    2. Click "Add Movie"
</details>

<details>
  <summary> How to View the Film Page </summary>
    1. To go to film page, click on "Details" next to the chosen movie. 
</details>

<details>
  <summary> How to create a Song to add to a Movie </summary>
    1. On the film page, click "ADD SONG TO SOUNDTRACK"
    2. Insert title of song, artist, and music video url from YouTube.
    3. Click "ADD SONG"
</details>

<details>
  <summary> Reviews </summary>
    1. Reviews may be created for each movie. Ratings may be chosen. If unhappy with your review, it   may be deleted.
</details>
